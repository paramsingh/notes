A closed formula is one that doesn't contain a $$\sum$$ or $$\prod$$.

Example:

$$
    a_n = \frac{3^n - 1}{n}
$$

A closed formula can have a the sum or product of a _fixed_ number of parts, but not a _changing_ number of parts.
