Introduction

RunAI ("we," "our," or "us") operates the RunAI mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using RunAI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

Strava Authentication Data:

  • When you sign in with Strava, we receive your Strava athlete ID, name, email address, and profile information
  • We store Strava access tokens to sync your running activities

Run Data:

  • Running activities you upload or sync from Strava, including:
    • Distance, duration, pace, speed
    • Heart rate data (average and maximum)
    • GPS route data and elevation
    • Cadence, power, and temperature data (when available)
    • Notes and RPE (Rate of Perceived Exertion) ratings you add
  • Training plans and scheduled runs created through the App

Chat History:

  • Conversations with your AI running coach
  • Questions, feedback, and training goals you share
  • Run-specific analysis requests

Subscription Information:

  • Subscription status and purchase history
  • Payment processing is handled by Google Play and RevenueCat (we do not store credit card information)

Automatically Collected Information

Device and Usage Data:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version and usage patterns
  • Error logs and crash reports

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  1. Provide AI Coaching Services:

    • Analyze your running data to provide personalized training advice
    • Generate customized training plans based on your fitness level and goals
    • Track your progress over time and adapt recommendations

  2. Improve the App:

    • Understand how users interact with RunAI
    • Fix bugs and improve performance
    • Develop new features

  3. Manage Your Account:

    • Authenticate your identity via Strava
    • Sync running activities from Strava
    • Process and manage subscriptions

  4. Communicate with You:

    • Send important updates about the App
    • Respond to your support requests
    • Notify you about subscription changes
